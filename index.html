What is the internet

This is a global computer network.

It consists of private public ,academic, business.

It is linked by a broad array of electronic wireless devices.

It carries avast range of information resources and services.

origin

The internet goes back as far as the 1960s to enable time sharing of computers.

In the 1970s it was the backbone for interconnection of regional academic and military networks.

In the 1980s backbone for a private funding for other commercial extensions led to worldwide participation in the development of new networking technology and merger of many networks.

In 1990s the linking of commercial networks and enterprises and marked the beginning of transition to the mordern internet.
